Too many people still don't benefit from therapy, and dropout rates remain high.
To address this challenge we present a **new paper** on **precision mental health**
focused on the **NOVA project** (*Navigating Outcomes via Analytics*), a multi-phase
translational program designed to bring precision mental health into routine
clinical services.

The work proposes combining **measurement-based care** with **predictive
analytics** and **artificial intelligence** to deliver the right intervention, at
the right time, for the right person.

The paper will be presented at **CIPIE 2026**, in Madrid (9–11 July 2026).

This work is supported by the Spanish **Ministry of Science, Innovation and
Universities**, the **European Regional Development Fund**, **Villanueva
University** and **Medea Mind**.
