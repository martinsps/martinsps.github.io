The **NOVA project** (*Navigating Outcomes Via Analytics*) kicks off at the
**PreMind** lab (Precision Mind Lab) of Villanueva University, led by Pablo Roca and
funded by the Spanish Ministry of Science, Innovation and Universities.

NOVA aims to implement **precision mental health** in real-world psychological
services, combining systematic measurement with data analytics to deliver the right
intervention, at the right time, by the right clinician, for each individual.
