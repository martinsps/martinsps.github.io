**Dr. Humberto Cruz Esparra (PsyD)** met with **Dr. Pablo Roca** and the team at
the **Precision Mind Lab** (PreMind) of Villanueva University (Madrid) to discuss
how **artificial intelligence** can drive more **personalized, predictive and
scalable** mental health care, while remaining grounded in ethics, culture and
real-world implementation.

> "The future of mental health will be built across borders."

Encounters like this strengthen the international outlook of the NOVA project and
the collaboration between teams working to bring precision mental health into
real-world psychological services.
