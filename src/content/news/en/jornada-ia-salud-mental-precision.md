Why do two patients with the same diagnosis respond so differently to the same
treatment? This is the question that opens the session **"Artificial
Intelligence and Precision Mental Health"**, organized by the **Precision Mind
Lab** (PreMind) and the **NOVA project** at Villanueva University, together with
**PsyPilot by Medea Mind**.

## Session details

- **Speaker:** Dr. Lorenzo Lorenzo-Luaces (Indiana University Bloomington), a
  leading voice in personalized mental health treatments, predictive models and
  artificial intelligence.
- **Date:** Monday, 1 June 2026, at 4:00 PM (Madrid time).
- **Venue:** Classroom 1.4, Building B, Villanueva University (Madrid).
- **Format:** in person and via live streaming across Iberoamerica, in Spanish
  (~60 minutes).
- **Registration:** free with prior sign-up.

The session is aimed at researchers, doctoral students, clinical psychologists
and professionals interested in the applications of artificial intelligence in
psychology.
