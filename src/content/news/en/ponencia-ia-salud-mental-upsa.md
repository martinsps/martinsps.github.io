**Pablo Roca** opened the **PRACTICUM training sessions** of the Faculty of
Psychology at the **Pontifical University of Salamanca** with the lecture
**"Artificial Intelligence for Precision Mental Health"**, at the invitation of the
Faculty and Alfonso Salgado.

The talk explored how to implement precision mental health in real-world settings,
its challenges and its potential, presenting the progress of the **NOVA project** and
**PsyPilot**, with the collaboration of David Cortejoso Mozo and the support of the
Official College of Psychology of Castilla y León (COPCyL).

The project is funded by the Spanish **Ministry of Science, Innovation and
Universities** and supported by **Villanueva University** and the **Medea Mind**
team.
