**Ms. Itzihuari Iratzi Montufar Burgos**, from the **National Autonomous University
of Mexico (UNAM)**, visited the research group to present the progress of her
thesis, titled *"Behavioral activation for women living with HIV who present
psychological symptoms related to suboptimal adherence"*.

Encounters like this strengthen the international outlook of the NOVA project and
the exchange between mental health research teams.
