The Spanish **Ministry of Science and Innovation**, co-financed by the **European
Regional Development Fund**, has awarded the R&D project **"Precision Mental Health:
Navigating Outcomes via Analytics (NOVA)"**.

The project aims to translate **precision mental health** into real clinical
practice, shifting from "what works on average" to **informed decisions for each
patient**. It relies on measurement, algorithms and artificial intelligence tools,
integrated naturally into professionals' workflows and without replacing clinical
judgment, always keeping the person at the center.

With thanks to the teams at **Villanueva University**, the **Precision Mind Lab**
and **Medea Mind**.
